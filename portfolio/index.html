Berghs: Casuals app

Online gaming can be demanding, with games often requiring great skill and devotion. Made for my Berghs UX course, Casuals is an app for people who want to play games together with others, but doesn't want to spend a lot of time or energy on. From sketches to wireframe, via prototyping to user testing, I tried making the flow intuitive and simple. The app shows who is currently online and looking for company!
